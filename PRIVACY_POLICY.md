# Privacy Policy for Bookmark Manager Pro

**Last updated: April 21, 2026**

## Introduction

Bookmark Manager Pro ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Extension handles your data when you use it.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

Bookmark Manager Pro operates entirely within your browser. All data processed by the Extension remains on your local device.

## Data Access

The Extension requests the following browser permissions solely for its core functionality:

- **bookmarks** — To read, create, edit, and organize your bookmarks. This data stays in your browser's local bookmark storage.
- **storage** — To save Extension settings, tags, and preferences locally in your browser. This data is not synced or sent anywhere.
- **tabs** — To interact with the currently open tab (e.g., bookmarking the current page).
- **windows** — To manage Extension window behavior.
- **downloads** — To export your bookmarks as a file when you choose to download them.
- **host permissions (<all_urls>)** — To display bookmark information (favicons) from various websites in the Extension popup.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using the `chrome.storage` API and the browser's built-in bookmark system. No data is sent to any remote servers, cloud services, or third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Bookmark Manager Pro does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with anyone, for any reason.

## Children's Privacy

Our Extension does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, our Extension is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and in our GitHub repository.
